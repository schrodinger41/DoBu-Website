Courses

Jiu-Jutsu

Jiu Jitsu, or BJJ, is a ground-focused martial art emphasizing leverage and technique. It enables practitioners to subdue larger opponents through joint locks and chokes. Widely respected for its practical self-defense applications, BJJ fosters fitness and mental discipline.

Karate

Karate, a Japanese martial art, focuses on striking with hands, feet, elbows, and knees. It emphasizes discipline, respect, and self-improvement. Training involves forms (kata) and controlled sparring (kumite), prioritizing precision. Beyond self-defense, it cultivates fitness, mental clarity, and personal growth.

Judo

Judo, founded by Jigoro Kano in the late 19th century, emphasizes throws and groundwork techniques, utilizing opponents' energy. It centers on balance, leverage, and timing, prioritizing maximum efficiency. Beyond sport, Judo fosters fitness, mental discipline, and mutual respect.

Muay Thai

Muay Thai, from Thailand, is a combat sport known for its fierce use of fists, elbows, knees, and shins. It's prized for its aggressive style, valued in sport and self-defense. Rigorous training builds fitness, mental strength, and self-improvement.

Kid's Courses

Kids Jiu-Jutsu

Kids Jiu Jitsu is a specialized program teaching essential techniques in a safe, engaging way. Emphasizing ground control and basic submissions, it promotes fitness, discipline, and confidence. Through interactive sessions, kids learn self-defense and important life skills like teamwork and respect.

Kids Judo

Kids Judo teaches throws, holds, and groundwork in a safe, focused environment. It builds fitness, coordination, and values like respect and discipline. Through the program, children learn self-defense and develop character and sportsmanship.

Kids Karate

Kids Karate teaches fundamental techniques in a controlled, encouraging environment. It builds fitness, discipline, focus, and respect. Through the program, children learn self-defense and develop confidence and character for personal growth.
